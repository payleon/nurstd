Question 1

What is the most appropriate nursing intervention for a patient experiencing acute pain?

  • Administer PRN medication without assessing pain level
  • Assess pain level using a standardized scale
  • Tell the patient to wait for the next scheduled dose
  • Notify the physician immediately
Correct Answer: B

Pain assessment using a standardized scale is the first step in pain management. This helps determine the appropriate intervention and establishes a baseline for evaluating the effectiveness of treatment.
